# Who We Are

Misraje Real Estate Partners is the practice of Karen and Jack Misraje, a mother-and-son partnership recognized as the #1 Two-Member Team in Coldwell Banker Global Luxury. Over more than three decades and over a billion dollars in closed transactions, they have represented buyers and sellers throughout Los Angeles, and they bring that experience home to the canyon streets of Fryman Estates in Studio City.

## The firm and the canyon

Fryman is defined by its setting. The ridgelines, the open space, the trailheads at Fryman Canyon, and the privacy those things buy give these homes a character that does not translate to a spreadsheet. The practice was not built in Fryman, but it has spent years working it closely enough to understand what that setting is worth and to whom, which is the knowledge that separates a confident sale from a hopeful one.

## What a two-person team actually means

There is no team of associates here, and no client gets passed along. Karen and Jack are the practice, and a Fryman buyer or seller works directly with both of them. That is a deliberate choice. Two principals can hold the whole picture of a transaction between them, divide the work by strength, and stay accountable to the client and to each other from first showing to final signature.

## Why the track record matters here

Sales in Fryman are infrequent and rarely interchangeable, so each one sets a reference point that the next will be measured against. Karen and Jack's record, earned across decades and every kind of market, is what lets them advise with conviction when the comparable set is thin and the cost of a misjudgment is real. It is experience applied to a specific place rather than borrowed from a general one.
