# Meet the Partners

Misraje Real Estate Partners: Fryman Estates Specialists in Studio City

## Decades of work in the streets below Fryman Canyon

The homes of Fryman Estates have a rhythm of their own, set against the canyon, the ridgeline, and the trailheads that draw people to this part of Studio City. Karen Misraje and Jack Misraje have spent decades learning that rhythm. A mother-and-son partnership and licensed California real estate brokers operating with Coldwell Banker, they hold the #1 Two-Member Team distinction in Coldwell Banker Global Luxury, and they bring that standing home to Fryman.

## Reading the Fryman micro-market

A Fryman sale turns on the things a raw comparable set tends to miss: canyon outlook, privacy, how a lot sits relative to the trail and the open space, and the premium buyers place on that setting. With more than thirty years of practice, Karen and Jack price and position these homes on evidence rather than guesswork. They have watched the streets around Fryman Canyon move through more than one cycle, and that history is what lets them tell an owner what the market will pay and, just as often, what it will not.

## The strength of the partnership

What sets Misraje Real Estate Partners apart is the balance between two principals who divide the work and check each other's judgment. Karen and Jack handle the detail of a Fryman transaction together, start to finish, without passing clients down a chain. In a market this specific, that combination of tenacity and care is what turns a sound listing into a closed sale.
